I was born here in Rexburg, Idaho, and I have lived here my entire life. I am the youngest in my family, and growing up, I have always felt close to them. I have two older brothers, who are twins, and a sister. My sister and I fought all the time, but she has always been there for me and has always been my best friend. Being the youngest can be difficult — watching all my older siblings grow up, get married, and move out sometimes made me feel alone. But I know that because of the strong relationship we share, we will always have each other and always come back home. And besides, one day I'll grow up, get married, and move out myself.
